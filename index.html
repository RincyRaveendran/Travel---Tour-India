Explore India

AGRA

Agra, Uttar Pradesh is one of the most famous cities in India. It is renowned for being home to one of the Seven Wonders of the world, the Taj Mahal.

GOA

Goa, India, is a tropical paradise known for its stunning beaches, rich culture, and lively nightlife. The state is a perfect blend of Portuguese and Indian cultures.

DELHI

Delhi, the city that defines India, offers the perfect blend of the country's modern and cultural heritage. This is manifested through the different architectural marvels.

MUMBAI

The colorful stories and fancy music-and-dance routines of Bollywood films began life in this city in the 1930s. Mumbai itself is as larger-than-life as the heroes of its movies.

UDAIPUR

Located around the green hills of Aravallis, Udaipur is a charming city, known for its lavish royal residences, picturesque lakes, and magnificent temples.
